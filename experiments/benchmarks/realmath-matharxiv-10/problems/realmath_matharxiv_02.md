# RealMath problem realmath_matharxiv_02

Source: http://arxiv.org/abs/2205.01013v3

Let $f: PG \to \mathbb{R}^{2}$ be a plane generic immersion. What are the congruences, modulo $2$ and $4$, of the sums $$\sum_{\gamma \in \Gamma_{5}(PG)} c(f(\gamma)), \quad \sum_{\gamma \in \Gamma_{6}(PG)} c(f(\gamma)), \quad \sum_{\gamma \in \Gamma_{9}(PG)} c(f(\gamma))$$ and $$\sum_{\gamma \in \Gamma_{8}(PG)} c(f(\gamma))?$$
