# RealMath problem realmath_matharxiv_05

Source: http://arxiv.org/abs/2412.16993v1

Let \(C_d\) be a Fermat curve of degree \(d>3\) and consider a set of \(d\) co-linear sextactic points on \(C_d\). The \(d\) tangent lines at these sextactic points are drawn, and the corresponding \(d\) hyperosculating conics are constructed. What is (i) the number of intersection points of the \(d\) tangent lines on the fundamental triangle, and (ii) the number of points on the fundamental triangle that lie on every one of the \(d\) hyperosculating conics?
