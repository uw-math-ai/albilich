# RealMath problem realmath_matharxiv_00

Source: http://arxiv.org/abs/2205.03791v1

What is the expression for the harmonic centrality \(\mathcal{H}_{P_2 \times C_m}(u_i, v_j)\) of any vertex \((u_i, v_j)\) in the direct product graph \(P_2 \times C_m\), expressed in terms of \(m\) and the harmonic numbers \(H_k\)? Provide the formula for the cases when \(m\) is odd and when \(m\) is even.
