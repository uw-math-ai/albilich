# RealMath problem realmath_matharxiv_07

Source: http://arxiv.org/abs/2412.16629v1

Let $E/\mathbb{Q}$ be an elliptic curve with additive reduction at an odd prime $p$, and let $i$ be an even integer satisfying $0 \leq i \leq p-2$. Suppose that the quadratic twist $E^F$ has either good ordinary or multiplicative reduction at $p$, that the $\mu$-invariant of $L_p(E^F,\omega^{(p-1)/2+i}, T)$ is $0$, and that the $\mu$-invariant of $\theta_{n,i}(E)$ is non-negative for all sufficiently large $n$. What are the values of $$\mu(\theta_{n,i}(E))$$ and $$\lambda(\theta_{n,i}(E))$$, for all sufficiently large integers $n$, in terms of $p$, $n$, and $$\lambda(E^F,\omega^{(p-1)/2+i})$$?
