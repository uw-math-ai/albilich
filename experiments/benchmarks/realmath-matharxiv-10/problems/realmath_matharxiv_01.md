# RealMath problem realmath_matharxiv_01

Source: http://arxiv.org/abs/2206.14250v1

Let $n\geq 1$ be an integer and consider the space of square integrable functions on $$S^{2n-1}$$ decomposed as $$L^2(S^{2n-1}) = \bigoplus_{p,q\geq 0} \mathcal{H}_{p,q}(S^{2n-1}).$$ For fixed integers $p,q\geq 0$, the space $$\mathcal{H}_{p,q}(S^{2n-1})$$ consists of harmonic polynomials of bidegree $$(p,q)$$ and is an eigenspace of the operator $$\Box_b$$.

(a) What is the eigenvalue of $$\Box_b$$ corresponding to $$\mathcal{H}_{p,q}(S^{2n-1})$$?

(b) What is the dimension of $$\mathcal{H}_{p,q}(S^{2n-1})$$ in terms of $p$, $q$, and $n$?
