# RealMath problem realmath_matharxiv_09

Source: http://arxiv.org/abs/2412.17324v1

Let \(\Pi_{k,l}\) be the highest weight representation of \(\mathrm{G}_2(\mathbb{C})\) with highest weight \(k\omega_1 + l\omega_2\), where \(\omega_1\) and \(\omega_2\) are the fundamental weights. At the unique conjugacy class \(\mathcal{C}_2\) of order 2, the character \(\Theta_{k,l}(\mathcal{C}_2)\) is given by a piecewise formula depending on the parities of \(k\) and \(l\). What is the explicit expression for \(\Theta_{k,l}(\mathcal{C}_2)\) in terms of \(k\) and \(l\), with the four cases corresponding to: (i) both \(k\) and \(l\) odd, (ii) both \(k\) and \(l\) even, (iii) \(k\) odd and \(l\) even, and (iv) \(k\) even and \(l\) odd?
