# RealMath problem realmath_matharxiv_03

Source: http://arxiv.org/abs/2205.06122v1

Consider the set \(\mathcal{K}_c\) of 2-bridge knots with \(c\) crossings, where chiral pairs are not counted separately. Express \(|\mathcal{K}_c|\) in closed form as a function of \(c\) for the following cases:

1. When \(c\equiv 0\pmod{4}\),
2. When \(c\equiv 1\pmod{4}\),
3. When \(c\equiv 2\pmod{4}\), and
4. When \(c\equiv 3\pmod{4}\).

What is \(|\mathcal{K}_c|\) in terms of \(c\) for each case?
