# RealMath problem realmath_matharxiv_08

Source: http://arxiv.org/abs/2412.19095v1

Let $G_1$ and $G_2$ be two graphs with orders $n_1$ and $n_2$, respectively, and let $G_1+G_2$ denote their join. What is the expression for the chromatic polynomial $\mu(G_1+G_2;x)$ in terms of $x$, $n_1$, $n_2$, and the chromatic polynomials $\mu(G_1,\cdot)$ and $\mu(G_2,\cdot)$?
