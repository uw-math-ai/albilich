## Problem

A group \(G\) is said to be **invariably generated** by \(a\) and \(b\) if
\[
G=\langle a^g,b^h\rangle
\]
for every choice of \(g,h\in G\).

Let \(p\neq q\) be fixed primes. Does every finite group embed into a finite
group invariably generated by an element of order \(p\) and an element of order
\(q\)?
