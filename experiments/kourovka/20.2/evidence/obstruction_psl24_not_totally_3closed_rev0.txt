Named theorem interface attacked: “PSL(2,4), the smallest nonabelian simple group of Lie type, could furnish a positive answer.” In the faithful action on the five points of the projective line, PSL(2,4) has order 60 and is 3-transitive. Therefore its orbits on ordered triples are exactly the five equality-pattern classes: all entries equal; exactly two equal in each of the three possible positions; and all entries distinct. Every element of S5 preserves each such class. Thus PSL(2,4)^(3)=S5>PSL(2,4); explicitly, (1,2) is an outside orbit-preserver. This eliminates PSL(2,4) but is not a refutation of the root existential question.
